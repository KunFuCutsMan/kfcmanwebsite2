## The Narco is dead

In the morning, an operation was made to capture the leader of a drug cartel named _Jalisco Nueva Generación_ (CJNG, for short), which apparently was successful. Its leader, "El Mencho" was taken to custody via a helicopter, where he died. Again, apparently.

I don't know well the history regarding the war against cartels, but I do know what happened after his (current) death. Absolute chaos.

You can look it up on youtube, basically there has been various attacks and blockades across cities and highways, mainly on the states of Jalisco and Guadalaraja. Apparently, something called _The Hydra Effect_ is hapenning at the moment, where beheading the head of the metaphorical hydra leads to new heads growing, as the leader that was attacked cannot take control of the body of the beast. I believe this event reveals how fragile organized crime really is, because the moment its leader died, the best they could do was _disorganized crime_ across the country, just to recover the fear the cartel lost when news broke out. I mean, what does a COSTCO have to do with crime?

Right now, our only hope is in God delivering justice on everything. Vengeance is mine, says The LORD.
